Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Sparky app ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address (if you choose to sign in with Apple), and relationship details you provide.
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including activities completed, ratings, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Information about your mobile device including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Personalize your experience
  • Sync your data across devices (if you enable cloud sync)
  • Communicate with you about the App
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity

Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data will be encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account, which is governed by Apple's privacy policies.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information and relationship data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your partner if you explicitly enable partner sharing features
  • With service providers who need access to such information to perform services on our behalf (like Apple for iCloud storage)
  • If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Choices and Rights

You can choose to:

  • Disable cloud synchronization in the App settings
  • Delete your account and associated data through the App settings
  • Opt out of notifications and reminders

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
