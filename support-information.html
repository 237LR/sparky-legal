2. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new activity in Sparky?

To create a new activity, tap the "+" button on the main screen, then select "Add Activity." Fill in the details such as name, category, and target completion frequency, then tap "Save."

How does partner sync work?

Partner sync allows you to connect with another Sparky user to share activity completions. To use this feature, both users need to have a premium subscription. Go to the "More" tab, select "Partner Sync," and follow the instructions to generate or enter a partner code.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you need to restore your premium subscription, go to the "More" tab, select "Premium Features," and tap the "Restore Purchases" button. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchase.

Can I export my data from Sparky?

Yes, Sparky allows you to export your activity data. Go to the "More" tab, select "Settings," then "Export Data." You can choose to export in CSV format, which can be opened in spreadsheet applications like Excel or Google Sheets.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, you need to do so through your Apple ID settings, not directly in the app. Go to your device's Settings app, tap your name, then "Subscriptions," find Sparky in the list, and tap "Cancel Subscription."

3. Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Ensure your app is updated to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Check that you have sufficient storage space on your device
  • If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app (note: ensure your data is backed up to iCloud first)

Data Sync Issues

  • Verify you're signed in to iCloud on your device
  • Check that iCloud Drive is enabled in your device settings
  • Ensure you have an active internet connection
  • Try toggling iCloud sync off and on in the app settings

Partner Sync Problems

  • Confirm both users have premium subscriptions
  • Ensure both users have the latest version of the app
  • Try generating a new partner code
  • Check that both devices have an active internet connection

4. System Requirements

Sparky requires the following:

  • iOS 15.0 or later for iPhone
  • macOS 12.0 or later for Mac
  • An active internet connection for cloud sync and partner features
  • An Apple ID for app installation and in-app purchases
